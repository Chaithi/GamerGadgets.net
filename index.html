Robert Thayer

Programmer, Gamer, Geek, Maker

Rob lives in Omaha, Nebraska with his partner Chelle. His hobbies include learning new programming languages, board games, video games, podcasting, playing viola, frisbee golf, and making things with a 3d printer or laser cutter.

Integration Engineer at PayPal by day, coder and podcaster by night. Rob has put together several tools for helping Monks in the World of Warcraft and table top players of Cyrus and Shadowrun.

Rob hosts two podcasts: Monk Meditationand Pile of Shame.
